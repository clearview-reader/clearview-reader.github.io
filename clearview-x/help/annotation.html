Annotations

Annotations are supported on PDF, EPUB, CHM and MOBI books.

Important Tip

Annotations in a book will not be saved to the original book file, Clearview save annotations in its own library database automatically.

Clearview was designed to be a pure ebook reader. With Clearview automatically saving all revisions separately, you read and make annotations freely, without the bother to worry about whether you should save the annotations to the original book file.

Annotate on PDF

You can make annotations to PDF books with types as Line, arrow, rectangle, ellipse, highlight, strike, underline, free text and comments.
